'Accident Prevention Systems for Lorries', Final Report from the Dutch Ministry of Transport (2009)

The Dutch Ministry of Transport has recently released the TNO Final Report, which contains the findings ofÂ  a large-scale field operational test of active driver assistance systems, so called accident prevention systems (APS), for lorries.

This large-scale study involved more than 2,400 lorries supplied by 123 companies and lasted 8 months. During this time a total of around 77 million kilometers of driving behavior were measured during normal daily driving on Dutch motorways.

Objective studies:
